## 💭My Growth as a Developer

I start from zero , I began coding back in **Grade 10 in Angola**, writing my first lines in **C and C#**. That early foundation gave me a head start, but my diploma at CPUT has transformed *how* I think.

What's changed most is my **mindset**. I've moved from writing code that just works, to thinking like a real-world developer , considering architecture, object-oriented design, and computing logic. I've fallen in love with **Java**, not just as a language, but as a way of structuring thinking.

Some of my biggest growth areas:
- 🧠 **Object-Oriented Programming** — designing systems, not just scripts
- 🔁 **Computational Logic** — solving problems systematically
- 🤝 **Teamwork & Leadership** — through group projects and youth volunteer work
- 📣 **Communication** — presenting technical ideas clearly and confidently

> *"I grew up coding in Angola, and now I'm learning to think like a developer. That shift from writing code to designing solutions — has been the most important thing this diploma has given me."*
